About Star Wars Jedi: Survivor

Star Wars Jedi: Survivor continues the thrilling journey of Cal Kestis, one of the last surviving Jedi Knights, five years after the events of Jedi: Fallen Order. Set in the dark times of the Empire's reign, this action-adventure sequel pushes the boundaries of storytelling, combat, and exploration in the Star Wars universe.

Key Features:

  • Expanded Force abilities and lightsaber fighting styles
  • Vast, diverse worlds to explore across the galaxy
  • Challenging enemies and epic boss battles
  • Deeper customization options for Cal, his lightsaber, and more
  • Intense narrative that expands the Star Wars lore

As Cal faces new threats and allies, players will experience a mature, cinematic Star Wars story that tests the limits of what it means to be a Jedi in a galaxy under Imperial control. With improved graphics, responsive controls, and an immersive soundscape, Star Wars Jedi: Survivor promises to be an unforgettable adventure for fans and newcomers alike.

Expanded Gameplay Details

  • Combat System: Players can now switch between multiple stance options, including dual-blade, crossguard, and blaster combinations, offering a more diverse and strategic combat experience.
  • New Force Abilities: Cal has learned new Force techniques, including the ability to tame and ride creatures, manipulate the environment, and even slow time.
  • Exploration: The game features larger, more open-ended levels with Metroidvania-style progression, encouraging players to revisit areas with new abilities to unlock secrets.
  • Character Progression: A deeper skill tree system allows for more customized playstyles, with branching paths for Force abilities, lightsaber techniques, and survival skills.
  • Companion System: Cal can now recruit and fight alongside AI companions, each with unique abilities that complement different playstyles.
  • Improved Traversal: New movement options include wall-running, grappling, and gliding, making exploration more fluid and dynamic.
  • Customization: Players can personalize Cal's appearance, lightsaber design, and even the look of his ship, the Mantis.
  • Side Quests and Activities: The game includes a variety of optional missions, puzzles, and mini-games that expand the lore and offer unique rewards.
